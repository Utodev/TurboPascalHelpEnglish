 Reserved Words
 ----------------

Reserved words have fixed meanings within the Turbo Pascal language: You
cannot redefine them.

In the Borland manuals and in this Help system, reserved words appear in
boldface type.

Turbo Pascal is not case sensitive, so you can use upper or lowercase
letters for these reserved words.
