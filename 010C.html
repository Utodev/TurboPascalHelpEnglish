 +------------------------+
 | [X] Symbol information |
 +------------------------+

[X] When checked, the Symbol Information
option enables the compiler to generate symbol
information.

Symbol information consists of tables that provide line numbers
of all declarations or references to symbols in a module.

When a program or unit is compiled with symbol information, the
ObjectBrowser can display symbol definition and reference information
in that module. (in BP and BPW)

Symbol Information is equivalent to {$Y+}.
