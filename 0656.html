 Line-Style and Width Constants
 -------------------------------
Use these constants with GetLineSettings and SetLineStyle:

 Line Styles:
  SolidLn   0
  DottedLn  1
  CenterLn  2
  DashedLn  3
  UserBitLn 4 (User-defined line style)

 Line Widths:
  NormWidth  1
  ThickWidth 3
