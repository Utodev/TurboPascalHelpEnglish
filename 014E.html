 +----------------------+
 | [X] Auto Indent Mode |
 +----------------------+

[X] When you check Auto Indent Mode, pressing
Enter in an Edit window positions the cursor
under the first non-blank character in the
preceding non-blank line.

For example,         +----- press Enter here
                     |     to put the cursor
                     |     under the i in if
  if A <= B then <---+
    DoSomething  <------ press Enter here to
                        put the cursor under
                        the D in DoSomething

This feature can help you keep your program
code more readable.

You can use Optimal Fill in conjunction with
Auto indent Mode.
