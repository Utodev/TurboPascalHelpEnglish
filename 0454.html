   /G Command-Line Option
 ------------------------
The /G command-line option tells the command-line compiler to generate a
.MAP file that shows the layout of the .EXE file.

The /G option must be followed by S, P, or L to indicate the level of
information in the .MAP file.
  - The /GS option outputs the Segment section of the .MAP file.
  - The /GP option outputs the Segments and Publics sections of the .MAP
    file.
  - The /GL option outputs the Segments, Publics, and Line Numbers sections
    of the .MAP file.
