 TDateTime (type)     (WinDos unit)
 -----------------------------------
Variables of TDateTime type are used in connection with the UnpackTime and
PackTime procedures to examine and construct 4-byte, packed date-and-time
values for the GetFTime, SetFTime, FindFirst, and FindNext procedures.

 type
   TDateTime = record
     Year,Month,Day,Hour,Min,Sec: Word;
   end;

Valid ranges are:
 Year   1980..2099
 Month  1..12
 Day    1..31
 Hour   0..23
 Min    0..59
 Sec    0..59
