 Constant address expression
 -----------------------------

A constant address expression is an expression that involves taking the
address, offset, or segment of a global variable, a typed constant, a
procedure, or a function.

Constant address expressions cannot reference local variables or dynamic
(heap-based) variables, since their addresses cannot be computed at compile
time.
