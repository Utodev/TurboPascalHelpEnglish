 | Error in Integer constant |
 +---------------------------+

The syntax of Integer-type constants is
defined in the chapter "Tokens"
in the Turbo Pascal Language Guide.

» NOTE: Whole real numbers outside the
maximum integer range must be followed by a
decimal point and a zero, like this:

  12345678912.0
