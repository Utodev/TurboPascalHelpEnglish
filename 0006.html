 - Clipboard Window
 -------------------
The Clipboard stores the text you cut and
copy from other windows.

The currently highlighted text is what will
be pasted when you choose Edit|Paste.

You can edit the text in the Clipboard
window.

When you highlight text in the Clipboard
window, then choose Cut or Copy, the selected
text immediately appears at the bottom of the
Clipboard window.
