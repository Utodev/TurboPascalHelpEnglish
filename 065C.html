 Justification Constants
 ------------------------
Use these constants to specify horizontal and vertical justification for
SetTextJustify.

   Horizontal Constant | Value
   ===========================
    LeftText           |   0
    CenterText         |   1
    RightText          |   2

   Vertical Constant   | Value
   ===========================
    BottomText         |   0
    CenterText         |   1
    TopText            |   2

Here's how each justification constant places the output text relative to
the output coordinates:

      TopText     TopText  TopText   TopText
      LeftText      CenterText       RightText
      BottomText    BottomText       BottomText
