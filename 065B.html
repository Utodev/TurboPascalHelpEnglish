 BitBlt Operators
 -----------------
Use these operators for images you place on the screen with PutImage.

 Constant  | Value | Meaning
 ========================
 NormalPut |   0   | MOV
 CopyPut   |   0   | MOV
 XORPut    |   1   | XOR
 OrPut     |   2   | OR
 AndPut    |   3   | AND
 NotPut    |   4   | NOT
