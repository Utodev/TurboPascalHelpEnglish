 Flag constants     (WinDos unit and Dos unit)
 ----------------------------------------------
The Flag constants (fXXXX) test individual flag bits in the Flags register
after a call to Intr or MsDos.

 Constant   | Value
 ====================
 fCarry     | $0001
 fParity    | $0004
 fAuxiliary | $0010
 fZero      | $0040
 fSign      | $0080
 fOverflow  | $0800
