 - Window|Zoom    (F5)
 --------------
Choose Zoom to resize the active window to
the maximum size.

If the window is already zoomed, you can
choose this command to restore it to its
previous size.

You can also double-click anywhere on the
window's title bar (except where an icon
appears), or on the Zoom box in the window's
upper right corner, to zoom or unzoom the
window.
