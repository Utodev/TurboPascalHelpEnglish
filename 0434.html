 Subexpression (...)
 --------------------
An expression within parentheses is a subexpression.

 (Expression)

Subexpressions are evaluated completely before being treated as a single
expression element.

Another expression can precede the subexpression. In this case, the result
becomes the sum of the values of the two expressions, with the type of the
first expression.
