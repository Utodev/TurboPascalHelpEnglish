 - Run|Step Over     (F8)
 ----------------
The Step Over command executes the next
statement in the current procedure.

It does not trace into calls to lower-level
procedures, even if they are accessible to the
debugger.

Use Step Over to run the procedure you are
currently debugging, one statement at a time,
without branching off into other procedures.
