 Color Constants
 ----------------
Use these color constants with SetPalette, SetAllPalette,
TextColor, and TextBackground:

 Dark Colors:
(Foreground & Background)
 ========================
 Black         0
 Blue          1
 Green         2
 Cyan          3
 Red           4
 Magenta       5
 Brown         6
 LightGray     7

 Light Colors:
(Foreground)
 =================
 DarkGray       8
 LightBlue      9
 LightGreen    10
 LightCyan     11
 LightRed      12
 LightMagenta  13
 Yellow        14
 White         15

For flashing (blinking) text foreground, Blink = 128.
