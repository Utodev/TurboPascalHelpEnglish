 | Invalid procedure or function reference |
 +-----------------------------------------+

You are attempting to call a procedure in an
expression.

A procedure or a function must be compiled
in the {$F+} state, and can't be declared
with inline or interrupt if it is to be
assigned to a procedure variable.
