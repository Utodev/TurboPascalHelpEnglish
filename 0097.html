 - Toggle Breakpoint  (Ctrl+F8)
 ------------------------------
With the Toggle Breakpoint command, you can
set or clear an unconditional breakpoint on
the line where the cursor is positioned.

When a breakpoint is set, it is marked by a
breakpoint highlight.
