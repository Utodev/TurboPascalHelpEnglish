 | Edit|Clear  (Ctrl-Del)
 | Clear (Help local menu)
 -------------------------
The Clear command removes the selected text
but does not put it into the Clipboard.

This means you can't paste the text as you
could if you had chosen Cut or Copy.

The cleared text is not retrievable.
