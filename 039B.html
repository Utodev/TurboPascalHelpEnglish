 | Too many symbols |
 +------------------+

The program or unit declares more than 64K
of symbols.

If you are compiling with {$D+}, try turning
it off.

» NOTE: This will prevent you from finding
run-time errors in that module.

You could also try moving some declarations
into a separate unit.
