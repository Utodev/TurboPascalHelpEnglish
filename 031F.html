 Built-in Assembler Predefined Type Symbols
 -------------------------------------------
In addition to any currently declared Pascal types, the built-in assembler
provides these predefined type symbols.

   Symbol | Type
   ================
   BYTE   | 1
   WORD   | 2
   DWORD  | 4
   QWORD  | 8
   TBYTE  | 10
   NEAR   | 0FFFEH
   FAR    | 0FFFFH
