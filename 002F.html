 |Edit|Cut  (Shift-Del)
 |Cut (Help local menu)
 -----------------------
The Cut command removes the selected text from
your document and places the text in the
Clipboard.

You can then choose Edit|Paste to paste the
cut text into any other document (or somewhere
else in the same document).

The text remains selected in the Clipboard so
you can paste it as many times as you want.
