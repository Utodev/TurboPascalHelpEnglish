  String-type constants
 -----------------------
The declaration of a string-constant specifies the maximum length of the
string and its initial value:

 Examples:
 const
   Heading: string[7] = 'Section';
   NewLine: string[2] = #13#10;
   TrueStr: string[5] = 'Yes';
   FalseStr: string[5] = 'No';
