 - Direction
 ------------
The Direction radio buttons specify the
direction you want Pascal to search--starting
from the Origin setting.

 +---------------+
 |  (.) Forward  |
 +---------------+
Forward searches toward the end of the file.
Forward is the default Direction setting.

 +---------------+
 |  ( ) Backward |
 +---------------+
Backward searches toward the beginning.
