 case (reserved word)
 ----------------------
The case statement consists of an expression (the selector) and a list of
statements, each prefixed with a case.

 Syntax:
 case expression of
   case: statement;
   ...
   case: statement;
 end

 OR

 case expression of
   case: statement;
  ...
   case: statement;
 else
   statement
 end

 Remarks:
A case consists of one or more constants or ranges, separated by commas.

The else part is optional.

 Example:
 case Ch of
   'A'..'Z', 'a'..'z':  WriteLn('Letter');
   '0'..'9':            WriteLn('Digit');
   '+', '-', '*', '/':  WriteLn('Operator');
 else
   WriteLn('Special character');
 end;
