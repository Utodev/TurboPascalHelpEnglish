 - Origin
 ---------
The Origin radio buttons determine where the
search begins.

 +------------------+
 | (.) From Cursor  |
 +------------------+
From Cursor starts the search at the cursor's
current position.

The search then proceeds either forward to the
end of the scope, or backward to the beginning
of the scope (depending on the Direction
setting).

 +------------------+
 | ( ) Entire Scope |
 +------------------+
Entire Scope searches either the entire block
of selected text or the entire file (no matter
where the cursor is in the file).

The scope is defined by the Scope radio
buttons.
