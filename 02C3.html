02C3
 
 +----------------------------------+
 | Invalid floating point operation |
 +----------------------------------+

One of the following floating-point errors
occured:

 » The real value passed to Trunc or Round
   could not be converted to an Integer
   within the Longint range (-2147483648 to
   2147483647).

 » The argument passed to the Sqrt function
   was negative.

 » The argument passed to the Ln function was
   zero or negative.

 » An 8087 stack overflow occurred.

For details on correctly programming the
8087, refer to the chapter "Using the 8087"
in the  Turbo  Pascal Programmer's Guide.
