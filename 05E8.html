 destructor (reserved word)
 ----------------------------
A destructor is a special method that disposes dynamic objects from the
heap.

 Syntax:
 destructor method

 Remarks:
A destructor must be declared with the reserved word destructor.(Destructors
can be used for other cleanup opeations as well.)
