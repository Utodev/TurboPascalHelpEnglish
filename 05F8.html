 goto (reserved word)
 ----------------------
A goto statement transfers program execution to the statement prefixed by
the label referenced in the statement.

 Syntax:
 goto Label

 Remarks:
The label must be in the same block as the goto statement; it is not
possible to jump out of a procedure or a function.

 Example:
 label 1, 2;
 goto 1
    .
    .
    .
 1: WriteLn ('Abnormal program termination');
 2: WriteLn ('Normal program termination');
