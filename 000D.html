 - Debugging in the Edit Window
 -------------------------------
Your program is running and the integrated
debugger is using the Edit window to step
through your source code.

By positioning the cursor, you can set
breakpoints, watch expressions, or evaluate
and modify variables.

Even though your program is running, you can
still perform normal editing operations,
including search and replace, opening and
closing files, and so on.
