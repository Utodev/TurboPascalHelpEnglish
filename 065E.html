 LineSettingsType
 -----------------
The record that defines the style, pattern, and thickness of a line.

 Declaration:
 LineSettingsType = record
    LineStyle : Word;
    Pattern   : Word;
    Thickness : Word;
  end;

 Remarks:
Refer to Line Style constants for a list of defined line styles and
thickness values.
