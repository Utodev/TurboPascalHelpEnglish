 Short-form headers
 --------------------
For short-form headers, type the reserved word (procedure or function),
 followed by the routine's name (identifier).

The routine will then contain all its local declarations (labels, constants,
types, variables, and nested procedures and functions), followed by the main
body of the routine itself.

Routines local to the implementation (not declared in the interface section)
must have a complete procedure/function header.
