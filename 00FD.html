 +---------------------+
 | [X] Word Align Data |
 +---------------------+

[X] When you check Word Align Data,  Turbo 
Pascal aligns non-character data at even
addresses.

[ ] When this option is off (unchecked),  Turbo 
Pascal uses byte-aligning, where data can be
aligned at either odd or even addresses,
depending on which address is available next.

Word alignment increases the speed with which
8086 and 80286 processors fetch and store the
data.

Word Align Data is equivalent to {$A}.
