 - Edit|Undo
 ------------
The Undo command takes back the last
editing command you performed on a line.

It can also take back a Ctrl-Y, or text typed
on a blank line.

Undo works on the last modified
or deleted line. However, you can press Undo
to keep "undoing" previous changes.

Set Group Undo in the
Options| Environment|Editor Editor Options
dialog box to Undo more than a single
keystroke.
