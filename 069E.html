 Origin (variable)     (WinCrt unit)
 ------------------------------------
Contains the virtual screen coordinates of the character cell displayed in
the upper-left corner of the CRT window.

 const Origin: TPoint = (X: 0; Y: 0);

Origin is a read-only variable; do not assign values to it.
