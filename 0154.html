 +-----------------------+
 | [X] Persistent Blocks |
 +-----------------------+

[X] When Persistent Blocks is on, marked
blocks remain marked even if you move your
cursor out of the marked block.

¯ Default = On

[ ] When this option is off, moving the cursor
out of a block of text de-selects the block.
