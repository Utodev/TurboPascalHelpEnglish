 Structure Member Operator (xxx.yyy)
 ------------------------------------
A second expression is a member of the structure identified by the first
expression.

 Expression1.Expression2

 Result:
The sum of the two expressions.

 Result Type:
The type of the second expression.

Symbols belonging to the scope identified by the first expression can be
accessed in the second expression.
