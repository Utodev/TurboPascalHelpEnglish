 File-mode constants     (WinDos unit and Dos unit)
 ---------------------------------------------------
File-handling procedures use fmXXXX constants when opening and closing disk
files.

The Mode fields of TFileRec and TTextRec will contain one of these values:

 Constant | Value
 ==================
 fmClosed | $D7B0
 fmInput  | $D7B1
 fmOutput | $D7B2
 fmInOut  | $D7B3
