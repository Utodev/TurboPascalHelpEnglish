  Printer Unit
 -----------------

The Printer unit (PRINTER.TPU) declares a text file called Lst and
associates it with the LPT1 device.

 var   Lst : text;

When you use the Printer unit, you don't need to declare, assign, open, and
close a text file yourself when you use your printer from within a program.
