The Text to Find input box is where you enter
the search string.

To begin the search, choose OK; choose Cancel
to forget it.

If you want to enter a string that you've
searched for previously, press the Down arrow
key to show the history list, then choose from
that list.

You can also pick up the word that your cursor
is currently on in the Edit window and use it
as the search string; simply invoke Find from
the Search menu.

To take additional characters from the text in
the Edit window, press the Right arrow key.
