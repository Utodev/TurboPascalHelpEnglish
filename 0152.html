 +--------------------------+
 | [X] Cursor Through Tabs  |
 +--------------------------+

[X] When you check the Cursor Through Tabs
option, the cursor moves uniformly through
the line as you press arrow keys for
horizontal movement.

[ ] If Cursor Through Tabs is not checked,
the cursor jumps several columns when you
move the cursor over a tab.
