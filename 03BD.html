 +--------------------------------------------+
 | Cannot add or subtract relocatable symbols |
 +--------------------------------------------+

The only arithmetic operation that can be
performed on a relocatable symbol in an
assembler operand is addition or subtraction
of a constant.

Variables, procedures, functions, and labels
are relocatable symbols. Assuming that Var is
a variable and Const is a constant, then the
instructions MOV AX,Const+Const and MOV
AX,Var+Const are valid, but MOV AX,Var+Var is
not.
