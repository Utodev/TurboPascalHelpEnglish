 Crt Unit
 ---------

The Crt unit (CRT.TPU, or CRT.TPP) implements a range of powerful
routines that give you full control of your PC's features.

The Crt routines provide control over screen mode, extended keyboard codes,
colors, windows, and sound.

Crt can only be used in programs that run on IBM PCs, ATs, PS/2s, and true
compatibles.
