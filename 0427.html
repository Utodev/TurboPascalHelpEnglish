 ST(x) Register Symbol
 ----------------------
The symbol ST denotes the topmost register on the 8087 floating-point
register stack.

Each of the eight floating-point registers can be referred to using ST(x),
where x is a constant between 0 and 7, indicating the distance from the top
of the register stack.
