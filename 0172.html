The Mouse Double Click slider bar adjusts the
double-click speed of your mouse. You use the
arrow keys to change the slider control bar.

Fast: If you move the scroll box closer to
Fast, Turbo Pascal requires less time between
clicks to recognize a double click.

Slow: If you move the scroll box closer to
Slow, Turbo Pascal will still recognize a
double click, even if you wait longer between
clicks.
