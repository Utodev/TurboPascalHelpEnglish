 | Options                                       |
 |  [X] Close on go to source                    |
 +-----------------------------------------------+

When this option is selected, the ObjectBrowser or Messages window will
close when you choose Go to Source.

If this option is not selected, the
ObjectBrowser or Messages window will remain open.
