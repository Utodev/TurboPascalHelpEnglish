 - Output Window
 ----------------
The Output window shows your program's output
(text mode only).

To devote the entire monitor screen to your
program's output, or to see your program's
graphics-mode output, choose the menu command
Debug|User Screen (or press the Alt-F5
hot key).

At startup, the Output window displays the
last screen from DOS.

You can pan through the Output window with
the cursor-movement keys.
