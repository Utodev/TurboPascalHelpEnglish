 - Debug|Add Watch   (Ctrl+F7)
 Û Add Watch command (Local menus)
 ----------------------------------
You use the Add Watch command to insert a watch
expression into the Watches window.

When you choose Add Watch, the debugger opens
the Add Watch dialog box and prompts you to
enter a watch expression.
