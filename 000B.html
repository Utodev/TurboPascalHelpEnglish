 - Call Stack Window
 --------------------
The Call Stack window shows the sequence of
procedures your program called to reach the
procedure that's currently running.

 » The currently running procedure is at the
   top of the stack.
 » "Program" (or your program name) is at the
   bottom of the stack.

Each entry on the stack displays the name of
the procedure called and the values of the
parameters passed to it.

To display the current line of any procedure
on the call stack, highlight it and press
Enter.

The Call Stack window will stay on the
desktop until you close it.
