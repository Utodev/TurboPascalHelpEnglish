 - Debug|Watch
 ---------------
Choose Watch to open the Watches window and
make it active. The Watches window displays
expressions and their changing values so you
can keep an eye on how your program
evaluates key values.

You use the command in the Watch local menu
menu to add or remove watches from this
window.

To close the window, click its Close box
or choose Window|Close.
