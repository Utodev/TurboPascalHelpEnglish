 Other 3.0 Graphics Procedures and Functions
 --------------------------------------------

 Procedure   | What It Does
 ===========================================================================
 GetPic      | Copies the contents of an area on the screen into a buffer;
             | contents can be restored later with PutPic
 GraphWindow | Defines an area of the screen as the active window in any of
             | the graphics modes
 Plot        | Plots a point at the specified coordinates and in the
             | specified color
 PutPic      | Copies the contents of a buffer to the screen

 Function    | What It Does
 ===========================================================================
 GetDotColor | Returns the color value of the dot at the indicated location
